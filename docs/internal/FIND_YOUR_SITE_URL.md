# How to Find Your HELIOS Site URL

## In Cloudflare Pages Dashboard

### Method 1: Overview Tab
1. Go to your "helios" project
2. Click **"Overview"** tab
3. Look at the top - you should see:
   - **"Visit"** button or link
   - Or a URL displayed prominently

### Method 2: Deployments Tab
1. Go to **"Deployments"** tab
2. Find the **successful** deployment (green checkmark)
3. Click on it to see details
4. Look for the URL or "Visit" button

### Method 3: Custom Domains Tab
1. Go to **"Custom domains"** tab
2. You should see your default Pages URL listed there

## Your URL Should Be

Based on your project name "helios", it's likely:
- `https://helios-dhp.pages.dev`
- Or `https://helios.pages.dev`
- Or `https://helios-[random].pages.dev`

## Quick Check

In the Overview tab, look for:
- A "Visit" button (usually blue)
- Or text that says "Your site is live at: [URL]"
